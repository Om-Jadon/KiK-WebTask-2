ABOUT US
How We Started
In the vibrant heart of Kolkata, the tale of THE TASTE began with a shared passion for exceptional dining. Friends turned founders, Arjun Kapoor and Priya Sharma, envisioned a culinary haven where every dish narrates a unique story.
From spirited kitchen conversations to the search for the perfect locale, our dream unfolded with a commitment to quality and innovation. As construction dust settled, THE TASTE emerged as a testament to the power of friendship and the belief that extraordinary food creates enduring memories.
Today, our kitchen is a canvas where global flavors and local traditions converge, inviting you to join us in crafting the next chapter of our delicious journey. Welcome to THE TASTE, where every bite tells a story worth savoring.
